# We Ate — User Guide

---

## Getting Started

After logging in for the first time you'll see a setup screen. Enter your name and your daily nutrition targets (calories, protein, carbs, fat, and optionally fiber). If you want to track a second person — a partner, spouse, etc. — you can add them on the Settings page at any time.

---

## One Person vs. Two People

### With One Person

- The daily tracker shows a single column of meal slots — breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks
- Macro progress bars track just your intake against your targets
- The recipe library only shows the Single recipe type — split recipes are hidden since they're designed for two people
- The weight log tracks your weight only

### With Two People

- The daily tracker shows both people side by side, each with their own meal slots
- Each person's macros track independently against their own targets
- The recipe library unlocks the Split recipe type — use this for meals you cook together where each person eats a different amount or has different nutritional needs
- The weight log tracks both people separately

---

## Adding Meals

There are two ways to log food each day:

- **Add from recipes** — pick something from your saved recipe library. When adding, you can adjust the portion size (0.5, 1, 1.5, 2x etc.) and the nutrition is calculated automatically
- **Quick add** — enter a name and nutrition numbers on the spot without saving it as a recipe. Good for one-off items or anything you won't eat regularly

---

## Recipe Library

- **Single recipes** — a fixed portion with set nutrition. Good for packaged foods, protein shakes, snacks, or anything with a standard serving
- **Split recipes** — only available with two people. Lets you set separate ingredient amounts and nutrition per person for the same dish

---

## Settings

- Edit your name and daily nutrition targets any time
- Add or remove a second person
- Removing a second person will delete their data, so the app will warn you before doing so

---

## Weight Log

Log your weight in lbs from the weight log page. The trend line shows your progress over time. Each person has their own log.

---

## History

Past days are saved automatically at midnight when the tracker resets. You can view and delete past entries from the history log.
